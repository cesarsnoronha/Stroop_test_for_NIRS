Paradigm to be used with Psychopy to collection of NIRS data. 

THis display images with 2 categories of words:

Condition 1 - Congruent Block (Color of top word matches meaning of bottom word)
Condition 2 - Incongruent Block (Color of top word doesn't match meaning of bottom word)
